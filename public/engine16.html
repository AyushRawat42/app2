Engine Description

This 1975 Dodge 318ci V8 engine is the perfect powerplant for your Dodge 100 Series truck restoration or repair. The 318 was the workhorse of Dodge trucks throughout the 1970s, offering reliable performance and good torque for hauling and towing.

This particular engine has been professionally rebuilt to factory specifications, making it an excellent choice for collectors and restorers who want to maintain their truck's originality. The LA-series 318 features a durable design with a cast iron block and heads, hydraulic lifters, and a 2-barrel carburetor for dependable performance.

Ideal for D100, D150, and other Dodge 100 Series trucks from the 1970s, this engine provides the authentic look and feel that classic truck enthusiasts demand.

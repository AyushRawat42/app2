Engine Description

The Honda 2.0L DOHC i-VTEC engine is the performance-oriented variant of Honda's acclaimed K-series engines, found in sportier trims like the Civic Si and Acura RSX Type-S. These available units come from our inventory of low-mileage (30,000-60,000 miles) engines that have been thoroughly inspected and tested.

Featuring Honda's high-performance DOHC i-VTEC system, this engine delivers thrilling power with its 8,000 RPM redline and linear power band. The dual overhead cam design allows for more aggressive cam profiles and better breathing at high RPMs compared to SOHC designs.

This engine is perfect for performance enthusiasts looking to upgrade their Civic Si (2006-2011), RSX (2002-2006), or for swaps into older Honda/Acura vehicles. The K-series engines are legendary for their tuning potential, with many examples reliably producing over 300hp with forced induction.

Each engine comes complete with all essential components including intake manifold, fuel rail, sensors, and accessories (specific components may vary by unit). These are ideal for replacements or performance projects.

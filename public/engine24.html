Engine Description

The Honda Odyssey 3.5L V6 engine delivers exceptional power and smooth operation, making it the perfect choice for your minivan needs. These available units come from our inventory of low-mileage (30,000-60,000 miles) engines that have been thoroughly inspected and tested.

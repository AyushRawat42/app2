Engine Description

The Chrysler 200 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir II engine delivers an impressive balance of power and efficiency, making it an excellent choice for your sedan. These available units come from our inventory of low-mileage (30,000-60,000 miles) engines that have been thoroughly inspected and tested.

Featuring FCA's innovative MultiAir II technology, this engine provides precise control of the intake valves for optimal combustion efficiency. The result is 184 horsepower and 173 lb-ft of torque with excellent fuel economy. The Tigershark design represents Chrysler's commitment to advanced four-cylinder performance.

This engine is perfect for Chrysler 200 (2011-2017) replacements, offering the same smooth, responsive performance that made these sedans popular. The aluminum construction keeps weight down while providing excellent durability.

Each engine comes complete with all essential components including intake manifold, fuel system, sensors, and accessories (specific components may vary by unit). These are ideal for those needing to replace their Chrysler 200 powerplant with a low-mileage, reliable option.

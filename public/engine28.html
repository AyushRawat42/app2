SRT 392 HEMI Performance

The Dodge Charger SRT 392's 6.4L HEMI V8 represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated American muscle, delivering a staggering 485 horsepower and 475 lb-ft of torque. These available units come from our exclusive inventory of low-mileage (30,000-60,000 miles) SRT engines that have undergone rigorous inspection and testing to meet SRT performance standards.

The "392" designation (referring to 392 cubic inches) honors classic muscle car displacement while delivering modern performance technology. This HEMI features high-flow cylinder heads, forged internal components, and SRT-specific tuning to achieve its impressive output. The active dual-mode exhaust provides an aggressive soundtrack that perfectly complements the engine's brutal acceleration.

This engine is ideal for Charger SRT 392 (2015-2023) replacements or high-performance builds. The 6.4L HEMI's broad torque curve delivers explosive acceleration at any rpm, while its robust construction handles track use with ease. These engines are also prized for swaps into classic muscle cars, offering modern reliability with traditional HEMI power.

Each SRT 392 engine comes complete with all essential performance components including intake system, fuel rail, sensors, and SRT-specific accessories (specific components may vary by unit). These are turn-key solutions for your high-performance project.

This 2023 Ford F-350 7.3L "Godzilla" V8 engine delivers exceptional towing capability without the complexity of diesel systems. With 32,000 well-maintained miles from a fleet vehicle, this engine offers outstanding value for Super Duty owners needing reliable gasoline power.

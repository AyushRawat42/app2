Engine Description

This 2023 Ford F-450 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine represents the pinnacle of commercial-grade powertrain technology. With only 38,000 carefully maintained miles from a fleet-operated F-450, this engine delivers exceptional value for commercial operators, municipal fleets, and heavy-duty applications.

The third-generation 6.7L Power Stroke features a compacted graphite iron block, forged steel crankshaft, and dual overhead cams for maximum durability under load. This particular unit has been professionally inspected and includes all necessary components for installation in F-450 chassis cab or pickup applications.

With its massive 1,050 lb-ft of torque available at just 1,600 rpm, this Power Stroke diesel is ideally suited for the most demanding F-450 applications including tow trucks, utility vehicles, and construction equipment.

Engine Description

This 2023 Ford F-550 6.7L Power Stroke V8 diesel engine is specifically built for the most demanding vocational applications. With 42,000 carefully maintained miles from a municipal fleet F-550, this powerplant delivers exceptional value for commercial operators requiring reliable heavy-duty performance.

The third-generation 6.7L Power Stroke features industry-leading engineering including a compacted graphite iron block, forged steel rotating assembly, and dual overhead cams. This particular unit has been professionally inspected and includes all necessary components for installation in F-550 chassis cab applications, including the complete wiring harness and factory ECU.

With its massive 1,050 lb-ft of torque available at just 1,600 rpm, this Power Stroke diesel is ideally suited for F-550 applications including utility trucks, mobile cranes, dump trucks, and other vocational upfits where maximum durability and performance are required.

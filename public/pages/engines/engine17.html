Engine Description

This 1978 Dodge 360ci V8 engine is the perfect powerplant for your Dodge 200 Series work truck restoration or heavy-duty repair. As the larger brother to the 318ci, the 360 was the preferred engine for D200/W200 models that needed extra torque for towing and hauling.

This particular engine has been professionally rebuilt to factory specifications, with attention to the durability needs of classic work trucks. The LA-series 360 features a robust cast iron block and heads, hydraulic lifters, and a 4-barrel carburetor for improved performance over the 2-barrel versions.

Ideal for D200, W200, and other Dodge 200 Series trucks from the 1970s, this engine provides the authentic power that these heavy-duty classics were known for, with the reliability of a professional rebuild.

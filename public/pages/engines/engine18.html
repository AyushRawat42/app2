Engine Description

This 5.9L Cummins 12-valve turbo diesel engine is the legendary powerplant that came in Dodge 300 Series trucks from 1994-1998. Known for its bulletproof reliability and massive torque output, this mechanical diesel engine has become highly sought after by truck enthusiasts and commercial users alike.

This particular engine has been professionally rebuilt to factory specifications, with attention to the durability needs of heavy-duty truck applications. The 6BT Cummins features a cast iron block and head, forged steel crankshaft, and the simple yet effective Bosch VE rotary injection pump that makes these engines so reliable.

Ideal for Dodge D300/W300 and other 300 Series trucks, this engine provides the authentic power that made these trucks legendary workhorses, with the reliability of a professional rebuild. The mechanical nature of this 12-valve engine means no complicated electronics to fail - just pure diesel power.

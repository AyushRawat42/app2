Engine Description

This 8.0L Magnum V10 engine is the legendary gasoline powerplant that came in Dodge 400 Series trucks from 1994-2003. Designed as a heavy-duty alternative to diesel engines, this V10 delivers massive torque output while running on regular gasoline.

This particular engine has been professionally rebuilt to factory specifications, with attention to the durability needs of commercial truck applications. The Magnum V10 features a cast iron block and heads, hydraulic roller lifters, and multi-port fuel injection for reliable performance under load.

Ideal for Dodge D400/W400 and other 400 Series trucks, this engine provides the authentic power that made these trucks capable workhorses, with the reliability of a professional rebuild. The gasoline design means no diesel emissions equipment or DEF systems to maintain.

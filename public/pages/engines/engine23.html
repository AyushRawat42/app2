Engine Description

The Honda 1.8L SOHC i-VTEC engine is renowned for its exceptional reliability, fuel efficiency, and smooth operation. This particular engine comes from our inventory of low-mileage units (30,000-60,000 miles) that have been thoroughly inspected and tested.

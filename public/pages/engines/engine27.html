Muscle Engine Description

The Dodge Charger 5.7L HEMI V8 engine delivers authentic American muscle with 370 horsepower and 395 lb-ft of torque. These available units come from our inventory of low-mileage (30,000-60,000 miles) engines that have been thoroughly inspected and tested to ensure premium quality.

Featuring the legendary HEMI design with its hemispherical combustion chambers, this engine provides exceptional power and efficiency. The 5.7L HEMI includes advanced technologies like Multi-Displacement System (MDS) that seamlessly switches between four-cylinder and eight-cylinder operation for improved fuel economy without sacrificing performance.

This engine is perfect for Dodge Charger (2006-2010) replacements or performance projects. The HEMI's massive torque output provides thrilling acceleration, while its robust construction ensures long-term reliability. These engines are also popular for swaps into older Mopar vehicles due to their excellent power-to-weight ratio and aftermarket support.

Each engine comes complete with all essential components including intake manifold, fuel system, sensors, and accessories (specific components may vary by unit). These are turn-key solutions for your Charger restoration or performance upgrade.
